Welcome to [[EcoliWiki:About|EcoliWiki]],
[http://porteco.org PortEco's] (formerly EcoliHub) subsystem for community annotation.
Our goal for EcoliWiki is to generate community-based pages about
everything related to non-pathogenic ''[[Escherichia_coli|E. coli]]'', its phages, plasmids, and
mobile genetic elements. Yes, we mean everything...[[EcoliWiki:Wish List|in the long run]].
